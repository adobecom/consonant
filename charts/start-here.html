Your guide to the Consonant 2 operating model portal

This portal contains 8 interactive diagrams that map out how Consonant 2 works: from redesign demand through contracts, systemization, build, ship, and measure. Each diagram is clickable—explore nodes to see detailed responsibilities, tools, and outputs.

Recommended reading order: Start with the Redesign-driven Flow to understand why the system exists, then follow the C2 Technical Workflow to see how it runs end-to-end. The North Star Org shows who owns what, and the Token Pipeline proves we've thought through distribution. Authoring addresses real-world publishing friction, and the codegen workflows are optional "future acceleration" views.
